Privacy Policy for WiseFido BLE Config Tool

Last Updated: 2025-04-06

1. Data Handling

What We Process Locally:

  • Wi-Fi configurations (name, encrypted password)
  • Server connection parameters (address and port)

What We Never Do:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Track device location
  • Share data with any servers or third parties

2. Local Storage & Security

  • Storage Limit: Only your 5 most recent configurations are kept
  • Automatic Cleanup: Older entries are deleted when new ones are added
  • Security: Sensitive details are protected using industry-standard safeguards

3. Your Control

  • Delete Single Entry: Remove specific configurations via the app's history list
  • Clear All Data: Use the "Erase History" option in settings
  • Uninstall: All local data is permanently removed when you delete the app

4. Permissions

Required permission:

  • Bluetooth Access: Solely for communicating with your WiseFido hardware

Note: No location permissions are required on Android 12+ devices.

5. Contact & Support

For questions or data requests:
support@wisefido.com (Response within 48 hours)
